For after the first date


Modern dating is full of unanswered questions. When you meet someone new, it's hard to work up the courage to ask — and answer — hard questions. Am I into them? Are they into me? Is there something here?

That's where we come in. At Dater, we believe the most difficult conversations are also the most important ones. Our philosophy to dating is to keep it real, open, and brave — both ways. You up for it?

How It Works


Step 1: Ghost-me-not

Log a date. We recommend talking about it with your date first. (Tip: We make a good conversation topic on a first date.)

Step 2: Be open

Answer some questions about how your date went.

Step 3: Good luck

Once your both answer questions, take a look and see where you both stand.
